Biography

Hee Guan Khor received the Bachelor of Engineering degree in Biomedical Engineering from Chongqing University in June 2019 and the Master of Engineering degree in Biomedical Engineering from Tsinghua University in June 2022. He subsequently pursued doctoral training in Biomedical Engineering at Tsinghua University, with Ph.D. degree completion scheduled for June 2026. His academic work has focused on clinically oriented medical AI, including medical image analysis, radiotherapy intelligence, and image-guided intervention, and he is currently serving as a Medical AI Engineer at Ant Group.

Core Areas

Medical Image Intelligence

Research centered on medical image segmentation, deformable registration, multimodal fusion, and ultrasound image enhancement, with an emphasis on robust learning-based methods for clinically meaningful imaging analysis.

Radiotherapy and Image-Guided AI

Development of AI models for radiotherapy target delineation, treatment-oriented image understanding, and anatomically informed decision support in translational clinical workflows.

Interventional and Surgical Computing

Work spanning intelligent minimally invasive diagnosis and therapy, robotic perception, and image-guided intervention systems that connect computational modeling with procedural applications.

Research Translation and Engineering

Current efforts bridge academic medical AI research with practical engineering deployment, linking university-based innovation with product-oriented medical AI development at Ant Group.
