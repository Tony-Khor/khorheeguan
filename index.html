Biography

Khor Hee Guan received his B.Eng. degree in Biomedical Engineering from Chongqing University in June 2019 and his M.Eng. degree in Biomedical Engineering from Tsinghua University in June 2022. He subsequently completed his Ph.D. in Biomedical Engineering at Tsinghua University. His academic and professional work has centered on clinically oriented medical AI, with a focus on medical image analysis and AI applications for diagnosis and treatment. He is currently a Medical AI Engineer at Ant Group.

Core Areas

Medical Image Intelligence

Research centered on medical image segmentation, deformable registration, multimodal fusion, and ultrasound image enhancement, with an emphasis on robust learning-based methods for clinically meaningful imaging analysis.

Multimodal AI for diagnosis and treatment

Development of clinically oriented AI frameworks integrating gene mutation data, CT, MRI, electronic health records, and other multimodal information to support diagnosis, treatment optimization, prognostic assessment, and decision-making in translational clinical practice.

Interventional and Surgical Computing

Work spanning intelligent minimally invasive diagnosis and therapy, robotic perception, and image-guided intervention systems that connect computational modeling with procedural applications.

Research Translation and Engineering

Current efforts bridge academic medical AI research with practical engineering deployment, linking university-based innovation with product-oriented medical AI development at Ant Group.
